Privacy Policy

Floor Plan & Carpet Calculator — Last updated: May 2026

✓ This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. What Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The app does not require you to create an account, provide your name, email address, or any other personal details.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

All data you enter — room names, dimensions, carpet settings, and saved plans — is stored exclusively on your own device using the browser's localStorage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is never sent to any server
  • Is never shared with any third party
  • Is fully deleted if you clear your browser/app data

3. Internet Access

The app requests internet permission solely to load the initial web assets when first installed. After that it works fully offline. It makes no network requests during normal use and does not communicate with any external server, API, or analytics service.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash-reporting services. No SDKs from Google Analytics, Firebase, Meta, or any other provider are included in this app.

5. Permissions Used

  • INTERNET — Required to load the web app on first launch only
  • Camera / Storage — Not requested or used
  • Location — Not requested or used

6. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, there is no special risk to younger users.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be available within the app and on the app's store listing page. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
